Gaelic Keepy Uppy — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Gaelic Keepy Uppy (the “App”) handles information. The App is offline-first and has no custom servers operated by the developer.

Information the App does not collect

The developer does not collect, store, or share any personal information about you. The App contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking. The App does not sell any data.

Information stored on your device

The App saves your game data — high scores, daily scores, coins, owned ball skins, selected options, and settings — locally on your device using standard on-device storage. This information never leaves your device through the developer and is removed when you uninstall the App.

Google Play Games Services

If you choose to sign in, the App uses Google Play Games Services for sign-in, leaderboards, achievements, and Cloud Save of your high score. When you use these optional features, Google may process a Play Games player identifier and your saved score on Google’s infrastructure. This data is handled by Google under its own terms, not by the developer. See Google’s Privacy Policy and the Google Play Games Services information for details. Sign-in is optional — if you skip it, the App runs fully offline and your scores are kept only on your device.

Permissions

The App requires network access only to sync with Google Play Games Services when you are signed in. No other sensitive device permissions are requested.

Children’s privacy

The App is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
